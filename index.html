Lionel Web

Welcome! I am Lionel Di Giacomo, an Automattic Happiness engineer and currently retired freelance web developer, digital mechanic, and tiny web host. I have worked with websites for twenty years and enjoy helping artists, nonprofits, and small heartfelt businesses get simple and functional websites up and running with minimal fuss.

I emphasize empowerment. I want you to be comfortable updating your own website. The web is a great publishing tool, but too many people get stuck with a website they can't edit themselves. With a little guidance up front, you can be in control of your content.

I support open-source. I prefer using open source platforms and software for most projects because they are durable, community-supported solutions. Additionally, by using popular technology my work can be more easily picked up and modified by future developers.

I often choose WordPress because it is popular, open source, and has loads of options. Among frontend frameworks, I enjoy Vue.js for its independent community, flexibility, and rapid growth. I also contribute issues, documentation, and sometimes development to open source projects (my github)

I can adapt to your project. I like learning new technologies and have worked on numerous projects with proprietary solutions like Shopify, Squarespace, Hubspot, and more.
