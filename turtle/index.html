Turtleverse

The programmer wasn't able to participate in this jam, so Turtleverse remains an unplayable art concept - for now!

The world is alive - literally! In Turtleverse, you steward a baby world turtle. You must give it food to keep it healthy and compel it to move through the universe and grow large enough to carry a whole world on its back. Without your guidance, your baby world turtle will wander, lost and hungry through the cosmos.
